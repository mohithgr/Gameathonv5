WHAT IS GAMEATHON?

Gameathon is an annual event where people get together and make a game over the 24 hours and this year......its going to be bigger!!


The event is organized by the Department of Information Science and Engineering, Jyothy Institute of Technology under BitsNBytes.
It is an annual exhilarating contest for beginners and veterans alike to develop their innovative ideas into an engaging game. This gives an opportunity to every eager gamer to learn and master the skills required to create their own masterpiece.

About the college

Jyothy Institute of Technology was established in the year 2011 under the leadership of Karnataka Rajyothsava Awardee, Dharmika Pravara, Late Dr. B.N.V Subrahmanya, visionary par excellence. Since inception, JIT has grown to be one of the reputed technical institutions in the city of Bengaluru. The main focus of our Institution is to empower the students with the kind of skill sets desired by the industry. This would help all students to compete in the highly demanding global industrial market. Our institution is blessed with a pool of highly qualified, talented, committed teaching and support staff to ensure a worthy learning environment for our students.
